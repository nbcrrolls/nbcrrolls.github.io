Video highlighting the Computer Aided Drug Discovery Pipeline is now available on YouTube and is viewable from the CADD page. CADD Pipeline is a workflow environment designed to support molecular dyanmics simulations and virtual screening experiments for in silico drug discovery, with a special focus on supporting the use of the Relaxed Complex Scheme.
