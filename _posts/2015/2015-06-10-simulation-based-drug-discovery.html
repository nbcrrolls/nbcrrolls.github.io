National Biomedical Computation Resource (NBCR) and the Theoretical and Computational Biophysics Group (TCBG) are pleased to offer a joint workshop on the theme of Molecular Sulation and Small Molecule Drug Discovery. This week-long intensive program will introduce 12-25 students to principles, methods, and NBCR / TCBG tools for simulating receptors (globular and membrane bound), small molecule docking, rigorous free energy calculations, and drug discovery strategies and workflows.
